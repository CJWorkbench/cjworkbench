Per capita

Introduction

Which city has more coffee shops: Seattle or New York? New York is a lot larger, so it almost certainly has more coffee establishments, but Seattle’s coffee culture means that there are more establishments per habitant.
Many numbers are best interpreted by comparing them to the overall population, giving a per-person rate. This is known as a per capita number (“capita” is Latin for “head”.)
